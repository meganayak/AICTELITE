AICTE Leadership in Teaching Excellence (LITE) is a national programme to equip a selected group of colleges and schools with leadership in teaching excellence.
AICTE with the help of some indutry leaders envisag to extend specialized leadership training for teachers from computer science and allied departments, to teach a minor degree programme in Web Development and skill their students using learner centered pedagogy, competency based curriculum and continuous assessments and professional development of faculty.

The aim is to impart modern software engineering knowledge through online teaching-learning methods to a larger cross section of Institutions, faculties and students so as to vastly increase the industry ready graduates in the field of IT across the nation. The trained faculty will have their leading role in this mission.
AICTE is implementing this programme in partnership with Pupilfirst.

The training of the first 100 faculty members from 50 institutions all across India has commenced on August 23, 2021. This site and associated activities are part of the work as a scholar of this course!
